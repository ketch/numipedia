4-step Adams-Bashforth

Explicit 4-step method of order 4

  • Back to index

Coefficients

\begin{align} y_{n + 4} - y_{n + 3} = \frac{55}{24}h f(y_{n + 3}) - \frac{59}{24}h f(y_{n + 2}) + \frac{37}{24}h f(y_{n + 1}) - \frac{3}{8}h f(y_{n})\end{align}

Properties

  • Order of accuracy: $4$
  • Stage order: 4
  • Radius of absolute monotonicity: $0$
  • $A(\alpha)$-stability angle: 0
  • Zero-stable: True
